Without You

I used to find happiness when watching the moon, especially when the person who used to accompany me would take photos of it. However, things have changed, and that person is no longer with me. As a result, the enchanting blue lights of the moon have lost their vibrancy for me. We used to share beautiful moments dancing under the moonlight on rainy nights, with our eyes locked together. I would see you in the shades of blue, and it felt like we were in a heavenly world illuminated by the moon's gentle glow. Do you remember how we would sit on that bench and I would sing songs just for you? But nowadays, I find myself spending sleepless days on that same bench, reminiscing about our time together. I realize that time is needed to heal myself, and that eventually, your memories will fade away. Someday, I will find happiness again while watching the moon, but it will be without you. Without you, the moon will still shine its light upon me. — Ek Doordarshi
