Ghar

Uske aane ke baad me apne aap ko bhul gaya hu, na jane is banjar zameen pe apna ghar leke aayi. Mere liye ab wo mera ghar hai. Uski khushbu pehli baarish me mitti ki khushboo jaisi hai. Usne mujhe pehchana jaise kisi ne dhool lagi hui kitaab ko saalon baad padha ho. Kyuki... ab mujhe ghar mil gaya. — Ek Doordarshi
