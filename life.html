Life

The act of pottery-making, childbirth, plant growth, and the expression of thoughts by writers – all of these actions bring about happiness. The process of a child's growth in a mother's womb over nine months mirrors a plant, where consistent care and nurturing are required to ensure their well-being and happiness. Our lives follow a similar pattern – pain may appear and disappear, but it is important to maintain a smile throughout every stage of life. We should smile like the innocence of a newborn child. Life can sometimes feel like an illusion, but don't be disheartened. In the end, illusions can become reality, just like those transformative moments. Keep your hopes up. Formulate a desire. Hold unwavering faith in its manifestation. — Ek Doordarshi
