Father

Today, I am writing about him because I'm afraid to tell him. He possesses superpower-like qualities, invisible strength, and an undefeatable positive energy. Most children, including myself, fear hugging him throughout their entire lives, even though they love him dearly. But this fear is baseless because he is a father — a superhero to some and even a god to others. Why are children afraid of him? His heart is as soft and beautiful as a flower, and his love is truly unconditional. He is the strongest person I know, capable of healing all my scars without even knowing me. The moment he places his hand on my shoulder is indescribable. I eagerly await that hug, and I am determined to find the courage to embrace him wholeheartedly. I will do it. I will. — Ek Doordarshi
