Overthinking

Sometimes, the loudest battles are the ones no one else can hear. A single thought becomes another. Then another. Until my mind creates storms from the smallest clouds. I replay conversations that have already ended. I imagine endings before beginnings. I fear things that have never happened. Overthinking steals today's peace by worrying about tomorrow's uncertainty. Yet, in the middle of all this chaos, I remind myself— the sky has never stayed covered forever. Every storm runs out of rain. Every night meets a sunrise. So today, I choose to breathe, to let go, and to trust that not every thought deserves my attention. Sometimes, the greatest act of courage is simply finding peace within your own mind. — Ek Doordarshi
