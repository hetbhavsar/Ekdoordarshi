Life

There are billions of stars spread across the universe, yet somehow, two souls still find each other. Perhaps, it isn't luck. Perhaps, the universe has always been writing our story. The galaxies may drift apart. The planets may continue their endless journey. The moon may rise and disappear again. But some connections are written beyond time. Beyond distance. Beyond explanation. Whenever I look at the night sky, I don't only see stars. I see memories, conversations, dreams, and the pieces of you scattered across the cosmos Maybe that's why I never truly feel alone. Because somewhere, between the stars, the moon, and the endless silence, our souls still remain cosmically close. — Ek Doordarshi
