Invisible Thread

Some people enter our lives without asking for permission. We don't know why, we don't know how, yet they slowly become a part of our soul. Even when distance grows, even when silence replaces conversations, something still connects us. An invisible thread, woven with memories, trust, hope, and love. No matter how far we go, it gently pulls us back to the people who truly belong in our hearts. Time may change us. Life may separate us. But some connections are stronger than distance, stronger than time, and stronger than words. Perhaps, that is what destiny looks like. An invisible thread that keeps two souls connected, even when the world tries to pull them apart. — Ek Doordarshi
